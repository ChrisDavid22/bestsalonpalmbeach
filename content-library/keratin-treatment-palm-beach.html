Understanding Different Smoothing Treatments

Brazilian Blowout

  • Duration: 10-12 weeks

  • Effect: Reduces frizz 80-90%, maintains some natural texture
  • Best For: Busy lifestyles, first-time treatment
  • Price Range: $275-$350 at Chris David Salon

Keratin Complex Treatment

  • Duration: 3-5 months
  • Downtime: 72 hours before washing
  • Effect: Reduces frizz 90-95%, more dramatic smoothing
  • Best For: Maximum longevity, very frizzy hair
  • Price Range: $250-$400 at Chris David Salon

Express Smoothing Treatment

  • Duration: 6-8 weeks
  • Downtime: 24 hours
  • Effect: Reduces frizz 60-75%, lighter smoothing
  • Best For: Fine hair, seasonal needs, budget-conscious

Important Treatment Considerations:

  • Wait Time: Keratin Complex requires 72 hours before washing - plan accordingly
  • Sulfate-Free Products: Must use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner after treatment
  • Swimming: Chlorine and salt water can reduce treatment longevity
  • Color Timing: Best to color hair 1-2 weeks before keratin treatment
  • Pregnancy: Consult doctor before treatment if pregnant or nursing

Maximizing Your Treatment Results

Before Your Appointment:

  1. Wash hair day of appointment unless instructed otherwise
  2. Arrive with clean, product-free hair
  3. Avoid hair color 1-2 weeks before treatment
  4. Plan for 3-4 hour appointment time
  5. Wear clothing that won't touch wet hair during processing

After Your Treatment:

  • Brazilian Blowout: Can wash immediately, resume normal routine
  • Keratin Complex: Wait 72 hours before washing, avoid ponytails/clips
  • All Treatments: Switch to sulfate-free products permanently
  • Swimming: Wear swim cap or wet hair with fresh water first
  • Heat Styling: Use heat protectant, lower temperatures work fine

Recommended Products:

  • Shampoo: Keratin Complex Smoothing Therapy Shampoo or Redken Frizz Dismiss
  • Conditioner: Keratin Complex Smoothing Therapy Conditioner
  • Deep Treatment: Olaplex No. 3 or Keratin Complex Deep Conditioner
  • Leave-In: Redken All Soft Argan Oil or Keratin Complex Infusion

Frequently Asked Questions

Will keratin treatment straighten my curls?

It depends on the treatment level and your natural texture. Keratin treatments can be customized from light smoothing (maintains natural wave/curl pattern with less frizz) to intensive smoothing (significantly relaxes curls). Discuss your desired result during consultation.

Is keratin treatment safe?

Yes, when using formaldehyde-free formulations applied by trained professionals in well-ventilated salons. Chris David Salon uses only safe, certified products with proper application techniques.

Can I color my hair after keratin treatment?

Yes, but wait 2 weeks after treatment. Ideally, color hair 1-2 weeks BEFORE keratin treatment for best results.

Will my hair be completely straight?

Not necessarily. Brazilian Blowout and lighter treatments maintain some natural texture while eliminating frizz. Keratin Complex provides more dramatic smoothing but can be customized.

How long does keratin treatment last?

Brazilian Blowout: 10-12 weeks | Keratin Complex: 3-5 months | Express: 6-8 weeks. Results vary based on hair type, aftercare, and lifestyle.

Can I get keratin treatment if I have color-treated hair?

Yes! Keratin treatments are safe for color-treated hair and can actually help preserve color by sealing the cuticle.
