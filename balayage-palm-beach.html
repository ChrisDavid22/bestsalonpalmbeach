Jupiter / Palm Beach Gardens

More relaxed, family-friendly atmosphere. Great options for natural-looking, low-maintenance balayage.

Price range: $175-400 | Top specialty: Natural blonde, beach waves

What Makes Great Balayage?

Seamless Blend

There should be no harsh lines. Color should melt from roots to ends naturally.

Face-Framing

Lighter pieces around the face brighten your complexion and add dimension.

Proper Tone

No brassiness or unwanted warmth. The toner should complement your skin tone.

Hair Integrity

Quality balayage doesn't sacrifice hair health. Your hair should feel strong.

Balayage Maintenance in South Florida

Our sun, salt water, and humidity require extra care:

  • Use UV protection: Sun fades color faster here
  • Pre-swim protection: Wet hair with fresh water before pool/ocean
  • Purple shampoo: Use 1-2x weekly to prevent brassiness
  • Regular toner refreshes: Every 4-6 weeks between full appointments
  • Deep conditioning: Weekly to combat humidity damage
