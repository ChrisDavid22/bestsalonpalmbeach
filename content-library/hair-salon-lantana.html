Balayage and Dimensional Color

Balayage remains the most popular color technique for good reason:

  • Natural Results: Hand-painted for seamless blending
  • Low Maintenance: Grows out beautifully without harsh lines
  • Versatile: Works with all hair colors and lengths
  • Beach-Friendly: Sun-kissed look perfect for coastal living

What to Expect:

A quality balayage service includes:

  1. Consultation: Discussion of desired look, placement, and tones (15-20 minutes)
  2. Application: Hand-painting of lightener with precision placement (60-90 minutes)
  3. Processing: Development time for optimal lift (30-45 minutes)
  4. Toning: Custom toner to achieve perfect shade (20-30 minutes)
  5. Styling: Cut, style, and final adjustments (45-60 minutes)

Total time: 3-4 hours for full balayage

Color Correction Services

Sometimes things go wrong - box dye disasters, brassy tones, uneven color. Professional color correction can save your hair:

Common Corrections Needed:

  • Removing box dye buildup
  • Correcting brassy blonde tones
  • Fixing uneven color distribution
  • Lightening previously darkened hair
  • Repairing over-processed hair
Before & After Photos: When consulting for color correction, bring photos of your desired result and your natural hair color. This helps your stylist create a realistic plan and timeline for achieving your goals.
