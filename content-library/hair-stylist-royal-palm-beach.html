Choosing the Right Stylist for Your Needs

For Color Services (Balayage, Highlights, Full Color):

Choose a stylist with:

  • Advanced color theory knowledge
  • Portfolio showing work similar to your desired result
  • Experience with your hair type and texture
  • Understanding of toning and maintenance
  • Olaplex or bond-building treatment integration

For Haircuts & Styling:

Look for:

  • Precision cutting technique
  • Experience with your hair texture (curly, straight, fine, thick)
  • Understanding of face shape and proportions
  • Ability to create styles that work with your lifestyle
  • Communication skills to understand your vision

For Complex Services (Color Correction, Transformations):

Essential qualities:

  • Extensive experience with corrections and difficult color
  • Honest assessment of what's achievable and realistic timeline
  • Patience and problem-solving skills
  • Knowledge of hair health and damage prevention
  • Willingness to schedule multiple sessions if needed

Making the Most of Your Salon Visit

Before Your Appointment:

  1. Book 2-3 weeks in advance for best availability
  2. Gather inspiration photos showing what you like
  3. Wash hair day of appointment (unless instructed otherwise)
  4. Arrive 5-10 minutes early
  5. Wear clothing allowing access to hair (avoid turtlenecks for cuts)

During Your Appointment:

  • Communicate clearly during consultation
  • Ask questions about process and products being used
  • Speak up if anything feels uncomfortable
  • Listen to stylist's professional recommendations
  • Relax and enjoy the pampering experience

After Your Service:

  • Ask for home care instructions and product recommendations
  • Schedule your next appointment before leaving
  • Take note of what you love (and anything you'd adjust next time)
  • Follow aftercare instructions for best results

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get my hair cut?

Short styles: every 4-6 weeks | Medium to long hair: every 6-8 weeks | Long hair maintenance: every 8-12 weeks. Frequency depends on style and growth rate.

How long does balayage last?

Quality balayage lasts 3-6 months depending on placement and maintenance. Root smudging extends time between appointments even further.

Should I tip my stylist?

Yes, standard tipping is 15-20% of service cost. For exceptional service, 20-25% is appropriate.

Can I request a specific stylist?

Absolutely! If you've had great results with a particular stylist, request them when booking future appointments.

What if I'm not happy with my service?

Communicate concerns immediately or contact the salon within 7 days. Reputable salons stand behind their work and will make adjustments at no charge.

Is the drive from Royal Palm Beach worth it?

For quality services, yes. At 25-30 minutes, it's a manageable drive that many clients combine with shopping or dining on Atlantic Avenue. The expertise and value justify the trip.
