Traveling from Palm Beach Island

Scenic Coastal Route (Recommended):

From Palm Beach Island, take Royal Poinciana Way to Flagler Memorial Bridge (southbound). Continue on Flagler Drive/Dixie Highway through West Palm Beach. Merge onto A1A South and continue through Lake Worth Beach to Delray Beach. Turn left onto Atlantic Avenue. Chris David Salon located at 403 E Atlantic Avenue on the right. Approximately 15-20 minutes with ocean views.

Efficient Highway Route:

From Palm Beach, access I-95 South. Exit at Atlantic Avenue (Exit 52). Head east on Atlantic Avenue for approximately 2 miles. Salon on right before Federal Highway. Approximately 15-18 minutes via highway.

Optimal Appointment Times:

Weekday mornings (9-11am) or early afternoons (1-3pm) offer the smoothest traffic flow. Weekend mornings also excellent. Advanced booking recommended.
