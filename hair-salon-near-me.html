How to Choose a Salon Near You

Consider Your Service

A trim or blowout? Location convenience matters most. Complex color? It's worth driving for expertise.

Read Specific Reviews

Don't just look at star ratings. Search reviews for your specific service (balayage, color correction, etc.).

Check Portfolios

Most salons post work on Instagram. Look for results similar to what you want.

Book Consultations

Many salons offer free consultations. It's the best way to assess expertise and salon vibe.
